Finding Us

Yes. It really is that easy to reach us! However, guests should know that there are 2 ways to get here via ferry or plane.

Directions via Plane

  1. Ensure ticket bought takes you to Volcaeverest, the flight time should take around 2 hours to get to your destination
  2. Upon arrival look for the Volcaeverest counter and book a ride. Once that is done, let our friendly staff brief you
  3. Now, just sit back and relax and be prepared! Or ask our friendly staff about the resort or the island!

Directions via Ferry

  1. Locate a booking counter that belongs to Volcaeverest. It should be located at the first row of counters, you can verify the counter by locating our resort name and staff number
  2. Upon arrival, have a chat with our personnel at the counter to book a ride and make sure to get a ticket back home as well as the waves tend to be unpredictable, you may want to be notify if such an event were to occur
  3. Now, you can either sit back and relax or enjoy the beautiful scenery as you drive through the island
