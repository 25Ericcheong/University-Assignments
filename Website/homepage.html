The Tale of Volca-everest

Launched in the year 2030, Volcaeverest was nothing but an ordinary erupting volcano - until 2 brothers came along and had the craziest idea to open a resort on the very tip of the unstable mountain!

What do we offer?

Thrilling activities? Exotic rooms? A combination of them both is what you are looking for? We have them all! Have a look at what we have or leave a message for our friendly staff members!

ACTIVITIES

Bungee Magma Jumping

Looking for some heat and excitement at the same time? This is the perfect activity for you! Thrilling, fast, and free (fall)! You can't find this anywhere else in the world!

Quad Lava Biking

Imagine taking a fully powered quad bike on a ride while having magma right by your side. It doesn't get much more thrilling and hot (quite literally) than this!

ROOMS

Relaxing Lava Simplicity

Would you want to wake up to the scene of flowing lava while on your bed? Look no further and look at our very own unique - Relaxing Lava Simplicity room! This room was created by our very own owners as well

Volcanic Lasting

Relax and be as close as possible as you can be to the flowing magma! You can be one with nature and experience real magma as well

FIND US

Via Ferry or Plane

Getting to us is as easy as walking to a park. Either take the plane or the ferry. Both being the same price and a relaxing one at the same time. Do not hesitate to contact us for more information on this
